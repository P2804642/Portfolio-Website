About Me:

Hi My name is Nathan Raynor and I am a freelance photographer, videographer and video editor. I am currently doing a degree in media production at De Montfort University in Leicester which is only helping further my skills and increase the quality of my work to an industry standard. While I do work in photography, my specialty is most definitely videography, more specifically cinematography and camera work on productions of short films. I have a keen interest and deep passion for media production which always lead to me striving for my work to be the absolute best work it can be.
